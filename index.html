Homework

Words where the first Kanji has the "Kan" sound:

  1. Kanpeki (完璧) - Perfect, flawless.
  2. Kanjiru (感じる) - To feel or sense.
  3. Kanren (関連) - Connection or relevance.
  4. Kantan (簡単) - Simple or easy.
  5. Kanji (漢字) - Chinese characters used in Japanese.

Words where the second Kanji has the "Kan" sound:

  1. Gokan (互換) - Compatibility.
  2. Shokan (書簡) - Letter or correspondence.
  3. Taikan (体感) - Bodily sensation.
  4. Reikan (霊感) - Sixth sense, spiritual intuition.
  5. Bunkan (分館) - Branch library or annex.
