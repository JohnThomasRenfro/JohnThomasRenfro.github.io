Memorizing Kanji
  • Use 10ten Japanese Reader browser extension to read kanji on your desktop/laptop computer.
  • Group kanji by the same sound using WaniKani.
  • See if you can notice any similar elements in the kanjis.
  • Practice writing the same sound kanjis until you can do it from memory using Android Akebi.

Homework

Practice in Akebi until you can memorize the 'Kyuu' Kanjis.
  • 休日 (Kyuujitsu) - Meaning "holiday" or "day off."
  • 緊急 (Kinkyuu) - Meaning "emergency" or "urgent."
  • 旧友 (Kyuuyuu) - Meaning "old friend."
  • 地球 (Chikyuu) - Meaning "Earth" or "planet Earth."
  • 宮殿 (Kyuuden) - Meaning "palace."
Five words that imply "very"..
  • 絶好 (Zekkou) - Meaning "perfect" or "ideal."
  • 超満員 (Choumanin) - Meaning "super crowded."
  • 最高 (Saikou) - Meaning "the best" or "fantastic."
  • 真新しい (Maatarashii) - Meaning "brand new."
  • 極寒 (Gokkan) - Meaning "bitterly cold."
